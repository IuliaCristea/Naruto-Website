About

Sasuke Uchiha

Naruto's rival, but also closest friend. He's an integral character in the Naruto world. He is Itachi Uchiha's younger brother. He is also a former member of Team 7. After defeating Itachi, he loses Orochimaru's curse mark and is told the truth of the Uchiha's destruction and of Itachi's secret life by Tobi.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno is a kunoichi from The Hidden Leaf Village. She is member of Team 7 along with Naruto, Sasuke and their team leader Kakashi. A ninja with uncanny chakra control and healing abilities. Sakura has quite a temper, and once angry her true power unleashes. Sakura is also one of Lady Tsunade's students.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is a Jonin of Konoha. He is the teacher of Naruto, Sakura and Sasuke. His teacher was the fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze and his teammates were Obito Uchiha and Rin Nohara. His rival is Might Guy. His father is the White Fang of Konoha. His one and only original technique is Raikiri.

Shikamaru Nara

Possessing a genious level intellect, he became the only student from The Leaf to become a Chunin during the period of rank leveling exams. To the discontempt of many who admire his potential, his probably the laziest and with more lack of enthusiasm ninja in his whole village.

Ino Yamanaka

A member of the Yamanaka clan. She's a kunoichi from The Hidden Leaf Village and Sakura Haruno's biggest rival in any matter of subjects. She is also a member of Team Asuma and is part of the Ino-Shika-Chou Trio. She is also a Medical-Ninja and the Daughter of Inoichi Yamanaka.

Choji Akimichi

Choji is a member of the Akimichi clan who use their size to their advantage. He loves eating and is very picky about the fact that he is fat. He is a reliable ninja and cares for his friends the most, notably Shikamaru Nara.

Kiba Inuzuka

A member of the Inuzuka clan. He combines ninja and feral atributes to compliment his overall ninja abilities, making him one of the best scouts in all of Konoha. He is a member of team 8 and is a Chunin rank leaf ninja.

Hinata Hyuga

A member of the main family of the Hyuga clan. Her soft and kind personality hides a strong will and determination, specially towards the person she cares the most.She has a tremendous crush on Naruto. So much so she often faints by merely being in his presence.

Shino Aburame

A member of the Aburame clan, an expert in ninja techniques that involve insects in them. A very mysterious and stoic person since childhood. He is a member of Team 8, alongside his teammates Kiba Inuzuka and Hinata Hyuuga.

Rock Lee

Self proclaimed Handsome devil of the leaf! and Konoha's beautiful green beast, this young shinobi is highly proficient at hand-to-hand taijutsu. Don't be fooled by his bushy-brow!

Tenten

Tenten is a fierce ninja of the Hidden Leaf Village and a member of Team 9. She's an expert in weapon based combat.

Neji Hyuga

Neji is a branch member of the famed Hyuga clan and also a child prodigy. He was widely regarded as the strongest young genin of his age group. Neji is on the ninja team with Rock Lee and Tenten.

Jiraiya

Jiraiya is one of the Three Legendary Sannin that is known through out the ninja world. Jiraiya is the teacher of many characters in the Naruto universe, including Naruto and the 4th Hokage. He is Naruto's Godfather.

Tsunade Senju

Tsunade is a kunoichi from the Hidden Leaf Village and posses monstrous physical strength, extremely advanced healing and regenerative techniques and great chakra control. At once formed part of The Legendary Sannin along with Jiraiya and Orochimaru. Currently, she is the Fifth Hokage of Konoha. Her grandparents are Hashirama and Mito.

Sabaku no Gaara

He was at once an enemy of Naruto Uzumaki but he befriends Naruto and eventually becoming the fifth Kazekage. He was once the jinchuriki of the One-Tail Shukaku, his tailed beast was extracted from him by the Akatsuki but was later given a second life by granny Chiyo at the cost of her own life.

Iruka Umino

The main teacher at the ninja academy, he grow up like Naruto as an orphan and thus acts like a father to Naruto. Iruka Umino is a Hidden Leaf Ninja with the rank of Chuunin. When the nine tailed fox attacked the Hidden Leaf Village both of his parents were killed trying to protect the village.

Kurama(kyuubi)

Kurama, more commonly known as the Nine-Tails, is one of the nine tailed beasts created by the Sage of the Six Paths. For centuries, it has always been feared but sought after to be used as a tool for war by countless humans. During its attack on Konoha, the Fourth Hokage sacrificed his life to seal the fox into his infant son, Naruto Uzumaki.

Killer Bee

He's a ninja from the Cloud village, the jinchuriki of Hachibi aka Eight-Tails, and uses Samaheda as his main sword. He loves music and uses a unique style of Kenjutsu.
